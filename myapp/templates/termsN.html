Our Services

We provide a platform to connect pet lovers with shelters, facilitate veterinary appointments, and share valuable pet care resources. Our mission is to promote pet adoption and responsible pet care through trusted partnerships.

Cookie Policy

We use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic. By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

Payments

Payments for veterinary appointments and pet adoption services (where applicable) are securely processed through Razorpay. We do not store your payment details, and all transactions are encrypted for security.

Vet Charges and Platform Fee

For each veterinary appointment booked through our platform, 85% of the total amount is transferred to the respective vet. The remaining 15% is retained by our platform as a service and operational fee. This ensures platform maintenance, customer support, and secure transaction handling.

Refund Policy

Refunds are applicable only for veterinary services in case of cancellation before the appointment. Refund requests must be made through our support team. Adoption-related donations or fees are non-refundable.

Data Privacy

We respect your privacy. Personal data collected during account creation, bookings, or inquiries is used solely for service delivery and communication purposes. We do not sell or share your information with third-party marketers.

User Responsibilities

Users must provide accurate information while registering or booking services. Misuse of the platform, inappropriate behavior, or fraudulent activities may result in account suspension or legal action.

Hyperlinking to Our Content

You may link to our website, provided that the link:

  • 2. Does not falsely imply sponsorship or endorsement.
  • 3. Fits within the context of your website.
  • 4. Does not portray us negatively.
