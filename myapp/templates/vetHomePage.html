Rules

Our Rules of Service

Appointment Timing

  • Vet must strictly follow the scheduled appointment time.
  • Failure to attend an appointment may result in account suspension.
  • Calling the user 30 minutes prior to the visit is mandatory.

Post-Appointment Report

  • After every visit, a detailed appointment report must be submitted.
  • Reports should include pet condition, diagnosis, and suggestions.
  • Report must be uploaded to the website within 12 hours of completion.

Payment & Withdrawal

  • Payments can only be withdrawn after the report is uploaded.
  • All withdrawals must be made through a registered bank account.
  • Processing time for payments is up to 72 hours.

Service Quality

  • Negative reviews from users will be investigated by the admin.
  • Repeated complaints may lead to penalties or account suspension.
  • Maintain professional behavior and hygiene during visits.
